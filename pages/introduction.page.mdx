# Introduction

**_haystraw_** is an API designed to streamline the process of checking individuals against PEP(Politically Exposed Persons) and sanctions lists from trusted sources, including governments, international organizations, and other political entities. Whether you are onboarding new users or conducting periodic checks, our API simplifies and automates this crucial task with a focus on simplicity, transparency, and reliability.

Whether you are a financial institution, a compliance platform, or any other organization requiring robust screening processes, _haystraw_ is here to support your efforts in maintaining compliance and security.
